I am currently a postdoctoral associate at the van Dijk Lab at Yale University. I completed my PhD in math in 2020 under the supervision of Jack Huizenga with a dissertation on moduli spaces of semistable sheaves. My current research interests are broad but include large language models and their applications to biomedical data, applications of topology to machine learning, and relational inference.
